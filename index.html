Michael Ranoia Poem 8/29/24

A Day Ago

My mind is stuck living in a yesterday

Too busy on the good times we shared

You're no good, that's what they all say

It's upsetting me because I always cared

I wish things were good between us

I wish we could start over and forget

When you look at me there is no trust

When I look at us there's only regret

The downfall was from an arrogant ego

I really miss you from so lomg ago
